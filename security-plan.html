Why this path — and why it skips Tier 1
Your profile — 5 years helpdesk, strong communicator, wired to chase mysteries — maps directly to Incident Response as an entry point, not SOC Tier 1. IR roles value operational context (you have it), structured thinking under pressure (you have it), and the ability to explain what happened (you have it). From IR you grow naturally into Threat Hunting (proactive IR), then Detection Engineering (automating what you hunt). This is the blue team specialist track — the exact path that commands $120k+ mid-career and never clicks an alert queue. Linux you'll learn in the context of investigating systems, which is completely different from memorizing commands for a cert.
